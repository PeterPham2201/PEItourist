Green Gables Heritage Place

In season, adult: $7.80, child (ages 6 to 16): $3.90.
A combo pass, which includes Green Gables and the site of L.M. Montgomery's Cavendish Home is $11.80 per adult and $5.90 per child. A large, free parking lot is available.

Visit Greengables main website

Adult - Person 18 to 64 years of age
Senior - Person 65 years of age or over
Youth - Person 6 to 17 years of age
Family/Group - Up to seven people arriving in a single vehicle in a national park
